Pasta e Piselli

Description

A simple dish that is easy, cheap to make and delicious to eat!

This dish consists mainly of two ingredients pasta and peas. Often made by italian families to get through rough times, it is a recipe that any one can make and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of any short pasta
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of dried mint
  • 1 fresh chilli
  • 1 tablespoon of tahini
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sea salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon of oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard
  • 3 pinches of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan. While it is warming up, slice the onion and garlic
  2. Pour the oil into the pan and add the sliced onion and garlic
  3. Heat up a saucepan with water for the pasta
  4. Once the onion and garlic are golden add the frozen peas
  5. Add seasalt into the water and pour the dry pasta in once water is boiling. Follow the instructions for the pasta, but turn stove off 3 minutes before the suggested time.
  6. Cut the fresh chilli into the pain and season with salt. Add some water to create a sauce and smash some peas with wooden spoon
  7. Add tahini, mustard and shave nutmeg into the pan
  8. Once the sauce has thickened slightly add the pasta in and cook together for 3 minutes and serve!
