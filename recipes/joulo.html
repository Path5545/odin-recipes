Joulo

Description

A hearty Nepali dish perfect for winter nights and sick days.

The picture above does not do the dish service, but I could not find an accurate image online

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of rice
  • 1/2 courgette
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1/2 cup of red split pea lentils
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 organic carrots
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 inch ginger
  • 1 tbs fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric powder
  • 1tbs sea salt
  • large bunch of fresh coriander

Steps

  1. First wash and prep all the vegetables. Rinse rice and lentils thoroughly in separate bowls.
  2. Turn on the rice cooker and heat up the pot, adding oil once warm
  3. Next add the fenugreek seeds once golden add chopped ginger and garlic. Adding chopped courgette and carrot once golden and releasing aromatics
  4. Add the chilli and tumeric into the pot and season with salt.
  5. Add the rice and lentils. Then cover with filtered water, the water should be 4 inches above the rice and lentils
  6. Let cook for 20 minutes
  7. Turn off the rice cooker once rice and lentils are cooked and liquid has thickened. Add the chopped coriander and serve warm.
