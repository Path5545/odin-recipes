Porridge

Description

An easy recipe for a quick and healthy breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of jumbo oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • Sprinkle of cinnamon
  • 1 clove
  • Sprinkle of ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • Soya milk
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 1 tablespoon coconut sugar

Steps

  1. First add the porridge into a small saucepan. Chop up the banana and place into the pan along with the frozen berries and soya milk
  2. Next turn on the stove and heat the pan for 6 minutes, making sure to stir occasionally.
  3. Once cooked, add the rest of the ingredients, stir well and serve!
